# Garden Privacy Policy

This repository contains the Privacy Policy for the Garden mobile application.

The Garden app is a hotel reservation platform that allows users to:

- Create an account
- Sign in securely
- Browse available room types
- Make hotel reservations
- Receive reservation confirmation emails
- Receive push notifications related to reservations

The complete Privacy Policy is available on the published website associated with this repository.
